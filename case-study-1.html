Optimization Automation with AirOps

Challenge:

The initial copywriting automation process was hybrid: it required significant manual input from the copywriter to provide context and product details to the AI, taking 15-20 minutes per optimization. This manual bottleneck forced the team to focus on repetitive execution instead of high-impact strategy.

Solution:

  • ✓

    Optimized AirOps Workflow with Minimal Input:

    Implementation of a custom AirOps solution that executes product-level SEO optimization end-to-end in seconds. The system only requires two core inputs: the target URL and the main Keyword.

  • ✓

    Automated Queue Processing and Quality Control:

    The workflow automatically processes defined queues of products/categories, applying high-quality, brand-aligned optimisation. This shifts team focus from repetitive execution to strategic tasks.

Results:

13 HOURS

Saved Monthly per Copywriter & Invested in Strategy and Professional Development
