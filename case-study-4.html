Strategic Growth in Adverse Conditions & Global KPI Standardization

Challenge:

The Rest of the World division faced significant market headwinds, resulting in a decline of -10%. Internally, the copywriting team lacked a standardized, data-driven KPI for optimisation, leading to scattered, low-impact efforts (only 5 transactions previously). The challenge was to simultaneously implement a hyper-efficient, data-validated KPI within a small team, and use that proven methodology to drive substantial year-over-year growth in six Spanish speaking markets, effectively decoupling performance from the divisional average.

Solution:

  • ✓

    Team-Led KPI Development and Validation:

    Developed and implemented a new KPI focused on High-Impact, Data-Validated Optimization Opportunities within the team, successfully shifting focus from volume to value.

  • ✓

    Automated G-Sheets Data Dashboard (GA4 & GSC Integration):

    Built a custom dashboard connecting GA4 data (transactional value) and GSC exports to automate the identification of the best optimization keywords. This tool was crucial for localizing strategy and isolating high-value markets for the WAG model. Key functionalities include:

    • Targeting: Segmenting all URLs by topic and monthly transactional value to provide clear SEO focus in managed markets.
    • Efficiency: A checker for bulk verification of pre-optimized URLs or keywords.
    • Quick Wins: Utilizing GSC data (rank, impressions, clicks) to easily detect low-hanging fruit and quick-win opportunities.

  • ✓

    Company-Wide KPI Standardization:

    The proven success and validation of the new data-driven KPI within the initial team led to its formal adoption and standardization across all Copywriting Teams, ensuring global strategic alignment.

  • ✓

    Culture-Driven Campaign Execution:

    Leveraged the data dashboard to guide high-impact, locally researched campaigns based on cultural and local market insights, ensuring maximum relevance and conversion in the six managed territories.
